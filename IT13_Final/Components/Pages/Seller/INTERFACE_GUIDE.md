# Seller Interface Guide

## Module 1: Dashboard

### Dashboard
The Dashboard Screen displays your business overview including total sales, net income, expenses, returns, supplier payments, and inventory statistics to help you monitor your SOFTWEAR business performance.

---

## Module 2: Product Management

### Product Record
The Product Record Screen lets you create, view, edit, and archive products in your SOFTWEAR inventory, including product names, categories, descriptions, and images.

### Category Record
The Category Record Screen lets you manage product categories to organize your inventory in the SOFTWEAR platform.

### Color Record
The Color Record Screen lets you add, edit, and manage color options for your products with hex color codes and visual previews in the SOFTWEAR platform.

### Size Record
The Size Record Screen lets you create and manage size options for your products to help organize inventory variants in the SOFTWEAR platform.

### Variant Record
The Variant Record Screen lets you create product variants by combining products with colors and sizes, and set pricing information for your SOFTWEAR inventory.

---

## Module 3: Supplier Management

### Supplier Record
The Supplier Record Screen lets you manage supplier information including contact details and payment records for your SOFTWEAR business.

---

## Module 4: Staff Management

### Cashier Record
The Cashier Record Screen lets you view and manage your cashier staff accounts who process sales transactions in the SOFTWEAR platform.

### Stock Clerk Record
The Stock Clerk Record Screen lets you view and manage your stock clerk staff accounts who handle inventory operations in the SOFTWEAR platform.

### Accounting Record
The Accounting Record Screen lets you view and manage your accounting staff accounts who handle financial operations in the SOFTWEAR platform.

---

## Module 5: Reports

### Accounting Income Breakdown
The Accounting Income Breakdown Screen displays detailed breakdowns of your sales income, including gross sales and revenue analysis in the SOFTWEAR platform.

### Accounting Expense Reports
The Accounting Expense Reports Screen shows all business expenses and operating costs to help you track spending in the SOFTWEAR platform.

### Accounting Financial Reports
The Accounting Financial Reports Screen provides comprehensive financial summaries including income, expenses, and profit analysis for your SOFTWEAR business.

### Accounting Profit Loss Reports
The Accounting Profit Loss Reports Screen displays profit and loss statements showing your business profitability over selected time periods in the SOFTWEAR platform.

### Accounting Tax Reports
The Accounting Tax Reports Screen shows VAT calculations, admin fees, and total tax obligations for your SOFTWEAR business.

### Accounting Daily Sales Verification Reports
The Accounting Daily Sales Verification Reports Screen lets you review and verify daily sales transactions submitted by your cashiers in the SOFTWEAR platform.

### Accounting Cash In Out Audit
The Accounting Cash In Out Audit Screen displays cash flow transactions including cash inflows and outflows for financial tracking in the SOFTWEAR platform.

### Cashier Sales Reports
The Cashier Sales Reports Screen shows detailed sales transaction reports from your cashiers including sales amounts and transaction history in the SOFTWEAR platform.

### Cashier Returns Reports
The Cashier Returns Reports Screen displays product return transactions and refund information processed by your cashiers in the SOFTWEAR platform.

### Stock Clerk Purchase Order Reports
The Stock Clerk Purchase Order Reports Screen shows purchase orders created by your stock clerks for inventory procurement in the SOFTWEAR platform.

### Stock Clerk Stock In Reports
The Stock Clerk Stock In Reports Screen displays inventory stock-in transactions showing when products are added to your inventory in the SOFTWEAR platform.

### Stock Clerk Stock Out Reports
The Stock Clerk Stock Out Reports Screen shows inventory stock-out transactions when products are removed from your inventory in the SOFTWEAR platform.

### Stock Clerk Stock Adjustment Reports
The Stock Clerk Stock Adjustment Reports Screen displays inventory adjustments made by your stock clerks to correct stock levels in the SOFTWEAR platform.

---

## Module 6: Settings & Logs

### Settings
The Settings Screen lets you update your personal information, address, email, password, and account preferences in the SOFTWEAR platform.

### Activity Log
The Activity Log Screen lets you view your personal activity history including all actions you've performed in the SOFTWEAR platform.

### Audit Logs
The Audit Logs Screen lets you view detailed audit trails of system activities and user actions for security and compliance in the SOFTWEAR platform.

---

## Module 7: Permission Management

### Permission Requests
The Permission Requests Screen lets you view and manage permission requests from your staff members in the SOFTWEAR platform.

---
