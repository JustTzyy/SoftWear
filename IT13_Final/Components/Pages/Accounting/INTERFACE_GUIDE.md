# Accounting Interface Guide

## Module 1: Dashboard

### Dashboard
The Dashboard Screen displays your financial overview including total income, expenses, profit and loss, cash flow, and pending approvals to help you monitor the SOFTWEAR business performance.

---

## Module 2: Financial Management

### Income
The Income Screen lets you view and manage income breakdowns including gross sales, returns, and net income calculations in the SOFTWEAR platform.

### Expenses
The Expenses Screen lets you create, view, edit, and manage business expenses including operating costs and expense categories in the SOFTWEAR platform.

### Supplier Payments
The Supplier Payments Screen lets you process and track supplier payment transactions for purchase orders in the SOFTWEAR platform.

### Petty Cash
The Petty Cash Screen lets you manage petty cash transactions and track small cash expenditures for daily operations in the SOFTWEAR platform.

---

## Module 3: Approvals

### Purchase Order Approvals
The Purchase Order Approvals Screen lets you review, approve, or reject purchase orders submitted by stock clerks in the SOFTWEAR platform.

### Refund Return Approvals
The Refund Return Approvals Screen lets you review and approve refund and return requests processed by cashiers in the SOFTWEAR platform.

---

## Module 4: Daily Sales Verification

### Daily Sales Verification
The Daily Sales Verification Screen lets you verify and approve daily sales transactions submitted by cashiers to ensure accuracy in the SOFTWEAR platform.

---

## Module 5: Reports

### Expense Reports
The Expense Reports Screen displays detailed expense reports with filtering options to analyze spending patterns in the SOFTWEAR platform.

### Sales Reports
The Sales Reports Screen shows comprehensive sales transaction reports including sales amounts and transaction history in the SOFTWEAR platform.

### Profit Loss
The Profit Loss Screen displays profit and loss statements showing business profitability over selected time periods in the SOFTWEAR platform.

### Tax Reports
The Tax Reports Screen shows VAT calculations, admin fees, and total tax obligations for financial compliance in the SOFTWEAR platform.

### Cashflow Audit
The Cashflow Audit Screen displays cash flow transactions including cash inflows and outflows for comprehensive financial tracking in the SOFTWEAR platform.

### Daily Sales Verification Report
The Daily Sales Verification Report Screen provides detailed reports of verified daily sales transactions for record-keeping in the SOFTWEAR platform.

---

## Module 6: Settings & Logs

### Settings
The Settings Screen lets you update your personal information, address, email, password, and account preferences in the SOFTWEAR platform.

### Activity Log
The Activity Log Screen lets you view your personal activity history including all accounting actions you've performed in the SOFTWEAR platform.

---

## Module 7: Archive

### Expense Archive
The Expense Archive Screen lets you view and restore archived expense records that have been removed from active records in the SOFTWEAR platform.
