# Stock Clerk Interface Guide

## Module 1: Dashboard

### Dashboard
The Dashboard Screen displays your inventory overview including stock levels, purchase orders, stock movements, and low stock alerts to help you manage inventory operations in the SOFTWEAR platform.

---

## Module 2: Inventory Management

### Inventory Record
The Inventory Record Screen lets you view and manage all inventory items including product variants, stock levels, and reorder points in the SOFTWEAR platform.

---

## Module 3: Purchase Orders

### Purchase Orders
The Purchase Orders Screen lets you create, view, and manage purchase orders for inventory procurement from suppliers in the SOFTWEAR platform.

### Completed Purchase Orders
The Completed Purchase Orders Screen lets you view purchase orders that have been completed and delivered in the SOFTWEAR platform.

### Cancelled Purchase Orders
The Cancelled Purchase Orders Screen lets you view purchase orders that have been cancelled in the SOFTWEAR platform.

---

## Module 4: Stock Operations

### Stock In Record
The Stock In Record Screen lets you record and manage stock-in transactions when products are added to inventory in the SOFTWEAR platform.

### Stock Out Record
The Stock Out Record Screen lets you record and manage stock-out transactions when products are removed from inventory in the SOFTWEAR platform.

### Stock Adjustment
The Stock Adjustment Screen lets you make inventory adjustments to correct stock levels for discrepancies in the SOFTWEAR platform.

---

## Module 5: Supplier Management

### Supplier Record
The Supplier Record Screen lets you view supplier information and contact details for purchase order management in the SOFTWEAR platform.

---

## Module 6: Reports

### Stock In Report
The Stock In Report Screen displays detailed reports of all stock-in transactions with filtering options in the SOFTWEAR platform.

### Stock Out Report
The Stock Out Report Screen displays detailed reports of all stock-out transactions with filtering options in the SOFTWEAR platform.

### Stock Adjustment Report
The Stock Adjustment Report Screen displays detailed reports of all inventory adjustments made to stock levels in the SOFTWEAR platform.

### Purchase Order Completed Report
The Purchase Order Completed Report Screen displays comprehensive reports of completed purchase orders for record-keeping in the SOFTWEAR platform.

### Supplier Report
The Supplier Report Screen displays supplier information and transaction summaries for supplier management in the SOFTWEAR platform.

---

## Module 7: Settings & Logs

### Settings
The Settings Screen lets you update your personal information, address, email, password, and account preferences in the SOFTWEAR platform.

### Activity Log
The Activity Log Screen lets you view your personal activity history including all inventory operations you've performed in the SOFTWEAR platform.
